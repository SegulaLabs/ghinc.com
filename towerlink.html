Overview

AI for Airport Operations

TowerLink develops AI-powered operations technology purpose-built for airports and airfields. By combining decades of aviation operational experience with advanced AI engineering, TowerLink delivers real-time conflict detection, intelligent transcription, and digital navigation — all without requiring complex infrastructure additions.

Aviation safety depends on precise communication and situational awareness. Runway incursions, readback errors, and navigation mistakes remain leading causes of aviation incidents worldwide. TowerLink's multi-layer AI platform integrates radar, video, radio, and ambient audio to identify risks before they become incidents.

As a GHI portfolio company, TowerLink brings its proven AI systems into the defense sector — including the Electronic Flight Strip built specifically for Air Force operational needs. The platform continuously evolves with new capabilities to stay ahead of emerging safety challenges.

Platform & Capabilities

A unified suite of AI tools that enhance safety, reduce workload, and improve decision-making across all airport operations.

Conflict Detection
AI Conflict Detection System

Multi-layer AI engine integrating radar, video, APIs, radio, and ambient audio to predict runway conflicts early and detect unsafe movement patterns — delivering prioritized alerts to tower and ground teams before incidents occur.

Aviation AI
Voice.Sense — Aviation Transcription

Real-time AI transcription built for aviation contexts. Accurately captures callsigns and phraseology despite radio noise and diverse accents, instantly flagging readback errors and mismatched clearances — addressing the leading cause of aviation miscommunications.

Ground Safety
Advanced Navigation System

Digital navigation for ground teams through complex taxiways. Replaces traditional radio communication with direct visual routing — preventing runway incursions and navigation errors while reducing voice congestion in the tower.

Air Force
Electronic Flight Strip (EFS)

Built for Air Force operational needs and formation control. Features real-time synchronization across units, secure continuous data logging with zero data loss, and full compatibility with existing legacy hardware.

Ground Operations
Ground Handling Management System

Digital pushback guidance providing accurate navigation and visual instructions to ground crews. Includes pre-movement safety briefings and records all actions for full transparency, consistency, and post-incident review.
