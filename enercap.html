Overview

What is Enercap Energy?

Enercap Energy develops non-lithium energy storage systems engineered specifically for the demands of defense and critical infrastructure. Unlike conventional lithium-based systems, Enercap's platforms eliminate fire risk, extend operational lifespans, and perform reliably across the extreme temperature ranges and duty cycles of real-world military deployment.

Working with Gabriel Holdings, Enercap successfully executed a Defense Innovation Unit (DIU) Other Transaction Authority (OTA) prototype project in partnership with the U.S. Air Force — a rigorous validation that confirms both technology readiness and the pathway to scalable defense deployment under Title 10 of the U.S. Code.

Enercap now stands at the inflection point between validated prototype and large-scale deployment, with confirmed purchase orders from Space Force and AFRL, and an active pipeline spanning Army, Navy, and Marine Corps programs.
