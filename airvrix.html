Overview

Eliminating FOD from Active Runways

Airvrix manufactures the world's only collapsible foreign object debris (FOD) barrier — a patented runway safety system that intercepts debris before it reaches active aircraft. The barrier folds flat for vehicle and aircraft crossings, delivering continuous protection without interrupting airfield operations.

Foreign object debris costs the global aviation industry $22.7 billion per year in direct damages, operational delays, and indirect costs. On military airbases, FOD incidents threaten high-value assets — jet engines, tires, fuselages — and mission readiness. Airvrix eliminates 90% of FOD incidents, independently validated through F-35 JPO testing.

As a GHI portfolio company, Airvrix benefits from defense relationships and government expertise that accelerate deployment from validated prototype to full-scale adoption across military and civilian airfields worldwide.
