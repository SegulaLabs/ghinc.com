About Us

Who We Are

Gabriel Holdings Inc (GHI) is a strategic holding and advisory company focused on deep-tech ventures at the intersection of defense, aerospace, and critical infrastructure. We identify, partner with, and scale breakthrough companies from early validation through large-scale deployment.

We go beyond capital. GHI brings government relationships, defense expertise, operational experience, and a network that opens doors — accelerating the path from prototype to program of record.

Our portfolio companies share a common thread: they solve real problems for real missions, backed by government validation and built for significant scale.
